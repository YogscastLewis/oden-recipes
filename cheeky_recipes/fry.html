Fry Recipe

We lack the R&D budget to attempt multiple French fries at once, but give it a try making this single fry!

  1. Find the finest potato
  2. Peal it
  3. Slice it into a fry shape using your sharpest knife
  4. Boil until stabbable with your dullest knife
  5. Half fill your pot with oil, and set it to boil
  6. Put fry in oil, deep fry until golden

Enjoy your delicious crunchy fry!
